Name: Cody Bloemhard

Occupation: CS Student, Utrecht University

Interests: Programming, Art, Computer Science

Born: 1998

Location: Utrecht, The Netherlands

Summary:

I have enjoyed programming since i was young. I used to make games in my free time. I studied Game Development at HKU for a year before i decided to study Computer Science at UU. I have made all kinds of things like 2D games, 3D games, game engines, graphics, experimental prototypes, interactive electronic devices, AI's and procedurally generated content. I support the philosophy, use and development of free open source software.
